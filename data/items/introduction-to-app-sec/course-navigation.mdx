The course layout is designed to make it easy for you to follow along and access the materials you need at every step.

#### Navigation Tips

- **Course Content Menu**: Use it to jump to specific sections.
- **Sequential Learning**: Follow the course modules in order for a coherent understanding.
- **Note-Taking**: Jot down important points as you progress.
- **Interactive Elements**: Engage with quizzes and exercises to test your knowledge.
