Throughout this course, we have explored **AI security from multiple perspectives**, covering key concepts, vulnerabilities, and mitigation strategies. The goal was to equip you with **practical knowledge** of AI security risks and how to defend against them.

### Major Topics Covered

1. **AI Security Fundamentals**

   - Understanding how AI systems function and where security risks emerge.
   - Core AI security principles such as **confidentiality, integrity, and availability (CIA)**.

2. **Threat Modeling and Secure AI Development**

   - Identifying **AI attack surfaces**, such as **data poisoning, adversarial examples, and model inversion**.
   - Implementing **security by design** principles for **robust AI systems**.

3. **AI Security Testing and Audits**

   - Conducting **penetration testing** on AI models to simulate real-world attacks.
   - Using **security audits** to assess compliance with industry standards.

4. **Incident Response and AI Monitoring**

   - Detecting security breaches in AI models through **real-time monitoring and anomaly detection**.
   - Responding effectively to AI-related security incidents and **performing post-incident analysis**.

5. **The Future of AI Security**
   - Examining **emerging AI threats**, such as deepfake attacks and AI-powered phishing.
   - The role of **AI-driven cybersecurity solutions** in strengthening defense mechanisms.

AI security is a constantly evolving field. Staying up to date with **new threats and defenses** is critical for securing AI applications in the long term.
