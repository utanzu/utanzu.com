AI security is a **rapidly evolving field**, and continuous learning is essential to stay ahead of emerging threats. Below are **recommended books, courses, research papers, and frameworks** to deepen your understanding.

### Books and Research Papers

- **"AI Superpowers: China, Silicon Valley, and the New World Order"** – Kai-Fu Lee
- **"Artificial Intelligence: A Guide for Thinking Humans"** – Melanie Mitchell
- **"Security and Artificial Intelligence: A New Frontier"** – MIT AI Security Report
- **NIST AI Risk Management Framework (AI RMF)** – [Read Here](https://www.nist.gov/itl/ai-risk-management-framework)
- **MITRE ATLAS: AI Threat Intelligence Framework** – [Read Here](https://atlas.mitre.org/)

### AI Security Tools to Explore

- **IBM Adversarial Robustness Toolbox (ART)** – Adversarial testing for AI security.
- **Google Model Card Toolkit** – AI transparency and bias mitigation.
- **OWASP AI Security Project** – AI application security best practices.

Continuous learning is **key to mastering AI security**. The more you explore, the better equipped you will be to **design, deploy, and defend AI systems** from cyber threats.
